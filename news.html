Current Event Summary

Why Is ICE So Aggressive Now? A Former ICE Chief Explains.

Rita Misra conducts an interview with a former ICE Chief explaining what makes the ongoing ICE raids so different from past years. Here's a summary of the article:

Christopher Meek argues that the current ICE workforce is severely hampered by a lack of adequate training. He describes a significant decline in the quality of recruits and a concerning reliance on outdated methods, leading to missed opportunities to disrupt criminal activity. Meek highlights that many new agents are ill-equipped to combat increasingly sophisticated smuggling operations and the evolving tactics of cartels. Ultimately, he believes this deficiency in training is a critical weakness that needs immediate attention to ensure effective border security and prosecution of serious crimes.

*As we see Christopher Meek's focuses on criminal activity not day to day people living their life in peace.
